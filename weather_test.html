Model constants

Wetness targets: DRY → 0.00  |  DRIZZLE → 0.40 (mid-damp)  |  RAIN → 0.78 (upper-wet)  |  HEAVY → 0.95 (flooding)
Wetting rate: exponential — DRIZZLE & RAIN: ×0.12/lap; HEAVY: ×0.24/lap (double)   (HEAVY reaches flooding from dry in ~7 laps)
Drying rate: linear — fixed −0.020 per lap   (≈12 laps per band; wet→damp, damp→greasy, greasy→dry)
Track bands: dry <0.08  |  greasy 0.08–0.30  |  damp 0.30–0.55  |  wet 0.55–0.80  |  flooding >0.80
Pace crossover (wets faster than slicks): ~0.50 wetness (top of "damp")
