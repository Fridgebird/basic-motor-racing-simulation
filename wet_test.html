HOW THE MODEL WORKS

Dry compounds (S/M/H) in rain:
weatherFactor = 1 + wetness × 0.18   (18% penalty at soaking)
tyreFactor = 1 + (1 − grip) × 0.06   (grip = effectiveMaxGrip × (1 − wear))

Wet compound in rain:
weatherFactor = 1 + (1−wetness) × 0.08 + wetness × 0.08   (8% penalty on both dry and soaking ends)
wetGrip = manufacturer_wetGrip × (0.55 + wetness × 0.45)   (worst on dry, best in soaking)
tyreFactor = 1 + (1 − wetGrip) × 0.06

AI switching thresholds (shouldPit):
Dry → Wet: trackWetness ≥ 0.55 − aggression × 0.20   (aggressive 0.35, conservative 0.55)
Wet → Dry: trackWetness ≤ 0.15 + aggression × 0.30   (conservative 0.15, aggressive 0.45)

Crash risk multiplier on wet track (applied to crash/slow thresholds):
Dry tyres: 1 + wetness × 1.5   (up to 2.5× at soaking)
Wet tyres: 1 + wetness × 0.3   (up to 1.3× at soaking)
